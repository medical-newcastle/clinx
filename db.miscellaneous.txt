## Miscellaneous, Opioid withdrawal %%
Management of Opioid withdrawal?
 - Anxiety: Benzodiazepines or Clonidine
 - Nausea: Promethazine or Diphenhydramine
 - Cramp/Pain: Non-opioid analgesia or Baclofen
 - Diarrhoea: Loperamide or octreotide
