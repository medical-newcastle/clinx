## Haematology, Lymphadenopathy %%
Causes of generalised lymphadenopathy?
- Lymphoma (rubbery)
- Leukemia (CLL, ALL)
- Malignant metastases
- Viral/protozoal infections (CMV, HIV, EBV, toxoplasmosis)
- Bacterial infections (Tuberculosis, brucellosis)
- Connective tissue diseases (rheumatoid arthritis, systemic lupus)
- Infiltrative disease (sarcoidosis)
- Drugs (phenytoin, pseudolymphoma)
